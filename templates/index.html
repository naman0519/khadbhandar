Our Agricultural Services

🌱 Quality Fertilizers

We provide trusted fertilizers such as Urea, DAP, NPK, Potash and SSP which are essential for crop growth. These fertilizers help plants get important nutrients like nitrogen, phosphorus and potassium. Using quality fertilizers improves plant health, increases crop yield and maintains soil fertility for long-term farming success.

🌱 Quality Fertilizers

Many farmers face problems deciding which fertilizer to use and when to apply it. We guide farmers about the correct fertilizer usage according to crop type and soil condition. Proper guidance helps farmers reduce fertilizer waste and achieve better production from their fields.

🧪 Soil Improvement

Healthy soil is the foundation of successful farming. We recommend balanced fertilizers and soil nutrients that help maintain soil fertility. Regular use of proper fertilizers and organic matter improves soil structure, increases nutrient availability and supports healthy crop growth.

🌾 Crop Nutrition

Different crops require different nutrients at different stages of growth. We help farmers understand the correct nutrient requirements for crops like wheat, rice, mustard and vegetables. Proper crop nutrition improves plant growth, strengthens roots and increases crop productivity.

Farmer Tips & Guidance

🌱 Best Time to Use Urea

Urea is mainly used to supply nitrogen to crops. It should be applied 20–25 days after sowing wheat or rice when plants start active growth. Applying urea at the right time improves leaf development, strengthens plants and increases crop yield.

🌾Benefits of DAP Fertilizer

DAP fertilizer contains phosphorus and nitrogen which are very important during early crop growth. It helps plants develop strong roots and improves seed germination. Using DAP at the time of sowing ensures healthy crop establishment.

🧪 Maintain Soil Health

Continuous farming without proper nutrients can reduce soil fertility. Farmers should use balanced fertilizers along with organic manure to maintain soil nutrients. Healthy soil produces stronger crops and improves long-term agricultural productivity.

🚜 Balanced Fertilizer Use

Using too much fertilizer can damage crops and soil health. Farmers should follow recommended fertilizer doses based on crop type and land size. Balanced fertilizer use improves crop quality, reduces cost and protects the soil.

Crop Wise Fertilizer Guide

🌾 Wheat

  • DAP at sowing time for root growth
  • Urea after 20-25 days for nitrogen supply
  • Second dose of Urea after 40 days

🌾 Rice

  • DAP during field preparation
  • Urea after transplanting
  • NPK for balanced crop nutrition

🌻 Mustard

  • SSP during sowing
  • Urea after 25 days
  • Potash for better flowering
